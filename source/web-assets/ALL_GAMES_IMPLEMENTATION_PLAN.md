## 🚀 **Implementation Order (by Complexity)**

### **PHASE 1: Easy Card Games** (Days 3-5)
**4. Go Fish** 🎣 - 1 day
   - Match cards by rank
   - "Go fish" when no match
   - Collect all 4 of a kind
   
**5. Crazy Eights** 8️⃣ - 1 day
   - Match suit or rank (similar to UNO)
   - 8s are wild
   - First to empty hand wins

**6. Blackjack** 🃏 - 1 day
   - Hit/stand vs dealer
   - Get to 21 without busting
   - Simple scoring

### **PHASE 2: Trick-Taking Card Games** (Days 6-8)
**7. Hearts** ♥️ - 1.5 days
   - Avoid hearts and Queen of Spades
   - Trick-taking game
   - Lowest score wins

**8. Spades** ♠️ - 1.5 days
   - Bid on tricks
   - Spades are trump
   - Partnership game (simplified for 2 players)

### **PHASE 3: Rummy-Style Games** (Days 9-10)
**9. Rummy** 🎴 - 1 day
   - Form sets (3-4 of same rank)
   - Form runs (3+ consecutive cards)
   - First to lay down all cards wins

### **PHASE 4: Simple Board Games** (Days 11-13)
**10. Checkers** 🟤 - 1.5 days
   - 8x8 board
   - Diagonal moves
   - Jump to capture
   - King pieces move backward

**11. Reversi (Othello)** ⚫ - 1.5 days
   - 8x8 board
   - Flip opponent pieces
   - Most pieces at end wins

### **PHASE 5: Dice Board Games** (Days 14-16)
**12. Ludo** 🎲 - 1.5 days
   - Roll dice to move pieces
   - Race to home
   - Capture opponent pieces

**13. Backgammon** 🎲 - 1.5 days
   - Roll 2 dice
   - Move pieces around board
   - Bear off all pieces first

### **PHASE 6: Complex Games** (Days 17-21)
**14. Chess** ♟️ - 3 days
   - 6 piece types with unique moves
   - Castling, en passant
   - Check/checkmate detection
   - (Simplified: no AI, just 2-player)

**15. Poker (Texas Hold'em)** 🃏 - 2 days
   - 2 hole cards + 5 community cards
   - Betting rounds (check/call/raise/fold)
   - Hand ranking (royal flush → high card)
   - (Simplified: fixed bet amounts)
