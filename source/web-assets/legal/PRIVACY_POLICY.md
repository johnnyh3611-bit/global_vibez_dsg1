## 7. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

### 7.1 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA)

**Right to Access:**
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Provided in machine-readable format

**Right to Rectification:**
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete data

**Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):**
- Request deletion of your data
- Subject to legal retention requirements

**Right to Restrict Processing:**
- Limit how we use your data

**Right to Data Portability:**
- Receive your data in structured format
- Transfer to another service provider

**Right to Object:**
- Object to processing based on legitimate interest
- Object to marketing communications

**Right to Withdraw Consent:**
- Withdraw consent at any time (does not affect previous processing)

**Right to Lodge a Complaint:**
- File complaint with supervisory authority

### 7.2 CCPA Rights (California)

**Right to Know:**
- Categories of data collected
- Sources of data
- Purposes for collection
- Third parties we share with

**Right to Delete:**
- Request deletion of personal data

**Right to Opt-Out:**
- Opt out of sale of personal data (we don't sell data)

**Right to Non-Discrimination:**
- Equal service regardless of privacy rights exercise

### 7.3 How to Exercise Rights

**Email:** privacy@globalvibez.com  
**Subject:** Privacy Rights Request  
**Include:** Full name, email, account username, specific request

**Response Time:** 30 days (may extend to 60 days for complex requests)
