## 📊 Pricing Comparison

### Example: 3-Mile Ride

**Uber/Lyft:**
- Base: $2.50
- Per mile: $1.50 × 3 = $4.50
- Per minute: $0.30 × 8 = $2.40
- Service fee: $2.50
- **Total: ~$12-15**
- Driver gets: ~$3-4 (25%)

**Global Vibes:**
- Base: $1.50
- Per mile: $1.20 × 3 = $3.60
- Per minute: $0.25 × 8 = $2.00
- Service fee: $1.80
- **Total: ~$9 (9 credits)**
- Driver gets: **$5.40 (60%)** + tips

**Result:**
- Rider saves: $3-6 per ride (20-40% savings)
- Driver earns: **$1.40-2.40 MORE** per ride despite lower price
- Platform earns: $3.60 per ride

## 📈 Business Model

### Revenue Sources:

1. **Ride Commissions** (40% of every ride)
   - Example: 100 rides/day × $9 avg × 40% = $360/day = $10,800/month

2. **Credit Packages** (Markup on purchases)
   - Users buy credits at premium (e.g., $10 = 12 credits)
   - Platform profit: ~10-15% markup

3. **Dating Premium Memberships**
   - Unlimited swipes, profile boosts
   - ~$20-30/month per premium user

4. **Gift Cards**
   - People buy ride credits as gifts
   - No refunds = guaranteed revenue

## 📱 User Flows

### For Riders:

1. Visit `/rides` landing page
2. Click "Book a Ride"
3. Sign up (Google Auth or email)
4. Buy credits (Stripe)
5. Enter destination
6. Get matched with driver
7. Track ride in real-time
8. Rate & tip driver

### For Drivers:

1. Visit `/rides` landing page
2. Click "Become a Driver"
3. Sign up & register vehicle
4. Upload documents
5. Wait for approval (~24-48hrs)
6. Go online in driver dashboard
7. Accept ride requests
8. Complete rides & earn 60%
