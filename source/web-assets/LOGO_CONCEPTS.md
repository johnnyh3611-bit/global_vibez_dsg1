## Logo Usage Guidelines

### Primary Logo
- Use on white backgrounds
- Maintain aspect ratio
- Minimum size: 32px height

### Color Variations
- Full color (primary)
- White version (for dark backgrounds)
- Black version (for print)

### Clear Space
- Maintain padding around logo
- At least 1/4 logo height on all sides
