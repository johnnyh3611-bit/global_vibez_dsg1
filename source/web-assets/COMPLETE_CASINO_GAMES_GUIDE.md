## 🎰 CASINO GAMES (21 Total)

### **Table Games** (8 games)

#### 1. **Roulette** 🎰
- **Players:** 1
- **How to Play:**  
  - Bet on numbers (0-36), colors (red/black), or groups (odd/even, high/low)
  - Dealer spins wheel, ball lands on winning number
  - Payouts: Single number (35:1), Color (1:1), Dozen (2:1)
- **Strategy:** Bet on outside bets (color/odd-even) for better odds
- **Badge:** ENHANCED

#### 2. **Baccarat** 🎴
- **Players:** 1
- **How to Play:**  
  - Bet on Player, Banker, or Tie
  - Two hands dealt (Player & Banker), closest to 9 wins
  - Cards: Face cards = 0, Aces = 1, others = face value
  - If total > 9, subtract 10 (e.g., 7+8=15 becomes 5)
- **Strategy:** Banker bet has slight edge (1.06% house edge)
- **Badge:** CLASSIC

#### 3. **Caribbean Stud Poker** 🏝️
- **Players:** 1
- **How to Play:**  
  - Ante bet to start
  - Receive 5 cards, dealer gets 5 (1 face-up)
  - Fold or Raise (2x ante)
  - Dealer must qualify with Ace-King or better
  - Win with better 5-card poker hand
- **Payouts:** Royal Flush (100:1), Straight Flush (50:1), etc.
- **Badge:** POKER

#### 4. **Three Card Poker** 🃏
- **Players:** 1
- **How to Play:**  
  - Ante bet, receive 3 cards
  - Fold or Play (match ante)
  - Dealer needs Queen-high to qualify
  - Win with better 3-card poker hand
- **Bonus:** Pair Plus side bet (pays regardless of dealer)
- **Badge:** POKER

#### 5. **Pai Gow** 🀄
- **Players:** 1
- **How to Play:**  
  - Get 4 tiles, arrange into 2 pairs
  - Each pair has point value (0-9)
  - Both pairs must beat dealer's pairs to win
  - One win, one loss = push
- **Special:** Traditional Chinese domino game
- **Badge:** ASIAN

#### 6. **Chemin de Fer** 🎩
- **Players:** 1
- **How to Play:**  
  - Baccarat variant where players take turns as banker
  - Bet on Player or Banker
  - Goal: Get closest to 9
  - Player can "take" or "stand" on 5
- **VIP Game:** High roller favorite
- **Badge:** VIP

#### 7. **Casino War** ⚔️
- **Players:** 1
- **How to Play:**  
  - Bet, then you and dealer each get 1 card
  - Higher card wins (Aces high)
  - Tie: Go to "war" (bet again, deal 3 cards, compare 4th)
- **Fastest:** Quickest casino game
- **Badge:** FAST

#### 8. **European Roulette** 🇪🇺
- **Players:** 1
- **How to Play:**  
  - Like American Roulette but NO double-zero (00)
  - Only single zero (0), 37 numbers total
  - Same betting options, better odds for player
- **House Edge:** 2.7% vs 5.26% (American)
- **Badge:** PREMIUM

### **Dice Games** (4 games)

#### 9. **Craps** 🎲
- **Players:** 1-8
- **How to Play:**  
  - Come-out roll: 7 or 11 = win, 2/3/12 = lose
  - Any other number = "Point"
  - Roll point again before 7 to win
  - Dozens of side bets available
- **Strategy:** Stick to Pass/Don't Pass for best odds
- **Badge:** DICE

#### 10. **Sic Bo** 🎲

- **How to Play:**  
  - Bet on outcome of 3 dice

- **Badge:** ASIAN

#### 11. **Hazard** 🎲

- **Badge:** CLASSIC
