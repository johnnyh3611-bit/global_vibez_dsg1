## 🎯 HOW TO SWITCH DESIGNS

### Table Center Logo:
1. Open: `/app/frontend/src/pages/games/BidWhistPremium.jsx`
2. Find the section: `TABLE CENTER LOGO - 4 DESIGN OPTIONS`
3. **Comment out** the current active design (wrap in `/* */`)
4. **Uncomment** your preferred design (remove `/* */`)

### Card Backs:
1. Open: `/app/frontend/src/components/bidwhist/ImperialCard.jsx`
2. Find the section: `CARD BACK DESIGNS - 4 OPTIONS`
3. **Comment out** the current active design
4. **Uncomment** your preferred design
