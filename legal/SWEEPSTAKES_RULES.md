Global Vibez DSG uses a two-tier virtual currency system. **These are not real money, crypto, or securities.**

- Gold Coins are a **virtual play currency** used for entertainment purposes.
- Gold Coins have **NO monetary value**, **cannot be redeemed** for cash, prizes, or anything of value, and **cannot be transferred** to another player.
- Gold Coins may be obtained through free daily bonuses, promotional offers, or optional purchase.
- Purchasing Gold Coins is for entertainment only — no prize, sweepstakes entry, or winning is tied to a Gold Coin purchase.

### 2.2 Sweeps Coins (Promotional Entries)
- Sweeps Coins are **free promotional entries** into our sweepstakes and tournaments.
- Sweeps Coins can be redeemed for eligible prizes (including cash equivalents) once redemption thresholds are met, subject to these Rules and identity verification.
- Sweeps Coins are awarded free of charge via the methods in Section 3.
- Sweeps Coins **are never sold** and are **never a direct consequence** of a Gold Coin purchase.
